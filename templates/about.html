About us

We’re a team of two students who realized that navigating academic and professional life can be isolating, especially without the right support. In Macedonia, it’s often hard to find mentors, or real-world projects to grow through. МОСТ was built to change that. Our platform connects students, encourages initiative, and creates a space where collaboration and local impact take the lead.

We built МОСТ because we wished it existed when we needed it.

Join us — and be part of a new wave of student-driven growth.
